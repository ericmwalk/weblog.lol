Bandwidth wastes no time from the first page dropping you right into the action as it captivates your imagination of a world not that far into the future. You will quickly figure out that the main character Dag Calhoon, a lobbyistÂ representing high-powered tech and energy executives, starts questioning the world he has been helping create. In this world, every person has a "feed" (like social media) that is always in people's minds and is integral to how the world works. As the story evolves, Dag stumbles upon a group of activists that have hijacked people's feeds in order to sway public options and global markets. He eventually is faced with big decisions as he tries to figure out what is right/wrong and whom to trust.
